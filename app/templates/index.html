About This Project

This project detects whether a pedestrian is distracted, especially by phone usage, by combining human pose estimation and phone detection in one structured MLOps pipeline.

  • MMPose detects people and extracts keypoints.
  • A posture model analyzes suspicious body posture.
  • A YOLO model checks cropped hand regions for phones.
  • FastAPI serves predictions and training control endpoints.
  • MLflow + DAGsHub track training runs and artifacts.
